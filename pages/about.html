Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Tools really free? +

Yes! All 50+ tools are completely free to use with no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no limits. We're supported by non-intrusive ads that don't interfere with your work.

Are my files uploaded to a server? +

No. All processing happens directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, and we have no way to access them. This is the most secure approach possible.

What's the maximum file size? +

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Most modern devices can handle files up to 100MB without issues. For larger files, try our compression tool first.

Do I need to create an account? +

Never. Just open any tool and start working immediately. No signup, no email verification, no passwords. We believe in respecting your time.

Does it work offline? +

Not yet, but we're working on it! Since all processing is client-side, we're developing a progressive web app (PWA) version that will work offline in the future.

Which browsers are supported? +

All modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, Brave, and Opera. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

How can I support PDF Tools? +

The best way to support us is to share PDF Tools with friends and colleagues! You can also help by not using ad blockers on our site ‚Äì the unobtrusive ads help keep everything free.
